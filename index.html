ABOUT

Four O'clock farms is an LBGTQ owned and operated small grains farm, specializing in red hard winter wheat as well as varieties of millet and sorghum. Built circa 1920, the farmhouse is constructed of several building salvaged from around Cheyenne County, including the former General Store and Post Office from Brownson, NE. Originally started by Donald Niece, the farm began as a humble 180 acre plot, but now spans over 1200 acres of high plains sandy loam, managed farmlands, and CRP grasslands.
